Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is our joy. We love to bring people out of their comfort zone, help them chase their adrenaline, do the big things. We take groups of anywhere from 2-20 people on guided whitewater rafting tours through the beautiful views of California.

Audience

The target audience is people who are looking for adventure. Maybe a date to go on with your partner. A family get together that isn't your typical reunion. Maybe you and some friends want to have an unforgettable boys trip. Or girls trip, we don't discriminate here.
